Why Gobbly

The chaos ends here

No more paper tickets

Orders go straight from the table to the kitchen screen. No handwriting, no lost tickets, no miscommunication.

Real-time kitchen sync

Kitchen staff see orders the moment they're placed. Status updates flow back instantly — pending, cooking, ready, delivered.

Customers pay their way

Diners scan the QR, pick what they ate, and split the bill however they like. Cash, card, or mixed — done in seconds.
